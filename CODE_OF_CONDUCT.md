# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Give and receive feedback in good faith
- Focus discussions on technical outcomes and evidence
- Respect privacy and security boundaries

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct
- Publishing private information without consent
- Personal attacks, trolling, or sustained disruption
- Sharing secrets or sensitive customer data

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or reject content, issues, comments, or contributions that violate this code.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to repository maintainers.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant.
